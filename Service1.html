MEDICINE & ALLIED

Our Medicine Department deals with diagnosis, treatment and prevention of diseases. Patients of all backgrounds are treated through the emergency department as well as referrals from all over the city. The Department of Medicine is fully supported by a Medical Intensive Care Unit (MICU) to treat critically ill patients who require intensive, round-the-clock care from a highly trained group of doctors, nurses, and other healthcare professionals. This Department has been providing high quality in-patient and out-patient health care since its inception in 1975. The Department provides a range of services including bronchoscopy, PFT, allergy tests, skin testing, PATCH test and vaccination under foreign qualified pulmonologist and allergist. The Department is resourced with 60 beds, state-of-the-art MICU, HDU and CCU, with specialists in various fields to provide quality health care services to in-patient and out-patient services seven days a week. The consultants are fully qualified with postgraduate degrees backed by extensive international experience in the field. The Department is also used as a teaching facility through a structured training programme for students pursuing their undergraduate and post-graduate degrees from Ziauddin University. Our department of Medicine deals in the sub-specialties of:

  • Gastroenterology
  • Nephrology
  • Neurology
  • Cardiology
  • Rheumatology
  • Pulmonology
  • Psychiatry
